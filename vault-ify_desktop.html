10 Unique Features

Everything banks don't tell you.

"We don't track subscriptions — we expose money you're losing without realizing it."

01👻
Ghost Detector
Usage vs. Cost Analysis
Compares your app usage against what you're paying. Flags subs unused for 3+ weeks as "ghost subscriptions."
"You paid ₹649 for a blank screen. That's 4 coffees in the trash."
02💧
Cumulative Drain
The Big Number Effect
Reframes every charge as its annual cost. Never shows ₹1.99/mo — always ₹23.88/yr. Droplets become puddles.
"₹87 × 14 times = ₹1,218/month 😳"
03⚡
Speed Breaker™
Intent-First Autopay
24 hours before any charge, you get a confirmation window. No response = autopay paused. Blind charges become conscious decisions.
"₹499 charges tomorrow. Tap to approve or we pause it."
04💰
Budget Cap
Monthly Subscription Limit
Set a max monthly budget for subscriptions. When the cap is crossed, new autopays pause and you're alerted. Intuitive for students.
"You've hit your ₹2,000 sub cap. New charges paused."
05😬
Guilt Score
Cost-Per-Hour Value Score
Each subscription gets a value score based on actual usage. Low-engagement subs get tagged as "High Guilt Autopay."
"Spotify: ₹299 → 2hrs used = ₹149.50/hr 😬"
06🔮
Regret Reminder
Future-Loss Framing
Shows future regret, not the current bill. Humans respond to annual loss much more than monthly cost.
"This ₹499/mo could become ₹5,988/year. Still want to continue?"
07😅
Adaptive Reminders
Tone Escalation Engine
Reminder tone changes when ignored. First time: gentle. Second time: calling you out. Rare in any app.
"You ignored this last month too 😅"
08🎓
Life Events
Context-Aware Alerts
Reminders trigger when your life changes: graduation, job change, city move. Subscriptions from your old life get flagged.
"You graduated — still need that student app?"
09🔍
Shadow Detector
Unlabeled Autopay Finder
Detects recurring charges not marked as autopay. Same merchant + same amount + same date = shadow autopay exposed.
"This behaves like an autopay but isn't marked as one."
10👥
Peer Compare
Anonymized Benchmarking
Shows how your subscription spend compares to similar users. Social proof is one of the most powerful behavioral nudges.
"People like you spend ₹620/mo. You spend ₹1,840."
